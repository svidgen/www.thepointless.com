## Explore the pointless dot-com (site navigation)

Welcome to the hub of pointless things. Here's a proposed top-level navigation for the site — quick links to the parts I think will suit the tone and intent of thepointless.com.

- Home — / (this page)
- About — /about.html (who we are and why the pointlessness)
- News & Feed — /feed.rss (RSS feed of site updates and headlines)
- Pointless Things — /features (short absurdist features and interactive bits)
- Apps & Games — /apps/ (progressive web apps and playable things; e.g. /apps/shooty-ship/)
- Words — /words/ (essays, shorts, and literary nonsense)
- Horoscopes & Signs — /hs/ (daily/weekly absurd horoscopes and personality quizzes)
- Books — /books.html (book project and writings)
- Contribute — /contribute.md (how to help, submit things, or donate)
- Archive — /archive/ (legacy content preserved for reference)

These sections can map to these repository locations:

- /apps/  -> src/ssg/apps/ (SSG-generated PWAs)
- /words/ -> src/routes/words/
- /hs/    -> src/routes/hs/
- /books  -> src/routes/books.html
- /feed.rss -> generated by src/ssg/feed.rss.ts (produces /dist/feed.rss)

A few notes about behaviour:
- Apps should remain served as PWAs (we keep their static assets under src/ssg/apps/). Directory requests should resolve to index.html (dev-server hosting fix pending).
- RSS and core pages should be SSG-generated so they exist under /dist for static hosting and CDN delivery.
